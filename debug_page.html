Introduction

The Department of Pharmacy at Iqra University, part of the esteemed Faculty of Pharmacy, offers a comprehensive academic program that prepares students for success in the field of pharmaceutical sciences. Our faculty members are dedicated to providing high-quality education, combining theoretical knowledge with practical experiences. With well-equipped laboratories and a vast library, students have access to state-of-the-art facilities to support their learning and research endeavours.

Our curriculum is designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills to thrive in the rapidly evolving pharmaceutical industry. We focus on practical training, internships, and industry collaborations to ensure students gain real-world experience.  

Research is at the forefront of our department’s activities, with faculty and students engaged in innovative research initiatives targeted at advancing pharmaceutical sciences and improving patient outcomes. Through our research efforts, we aim to contribute to developing new therapeutic approaches and address healthcare challenges.

Pharmacy

Iqra University Faculty of Pharmacy offers innovative and comprehensive academic programs that equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in the dynamic field of pharmacy. Our curriculum seamlessly integrates theoretical concepts with practical experiences through hands-on training, internships, and industry collaborations. With state-of-the-art facilities, well-equipped laboratories, and a vast library, we ensure an enriching learning environment for our students.

Furthermore, research is at the heart of our academic endeavours. We encourage our faculty and students to actively engage in cutting-edge research, leading to publications and discoveries that contribute to the advancement of pharmaceutical sciences. Our research initiatives aim to address global healthcare challenges and improve patient outcomes.
